Oblivion

  • Price: $30

  • Rating: 9.3/10 IGN

  • Developer: Bethesda

  • Release Date: 20th of March, 2006

Why Oblivion is one of my favourite games:

Oblivion, although it has poorly aged graphics, it still has exceptional writing with interesting quests and overall fun gameplay despite being a game originally from 2006.
Oblivion is also home to the horse armor DLC which was when the idea of DLCs started becoming popular.
This game has numerous memorable moments from dropping Adoring fans from large cliffsides with zero repercussions to having one of the most amazing DLCs. (The Shivering Isles)
