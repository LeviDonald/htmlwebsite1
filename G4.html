  • Price: NOT ON STEAM

  • Rating: 8.8/10 IGN

  • Developer: Atlus

  • Release Date: 19th of April, 2007

Why Persona 3 FES is one of my favourite games:

Persona 3 FES, the 3rd major addition to the Persona Series, is my 2nd personal favourite out of the Persona series.
The game revolves defeating a set of monsters which are based on card arcanas. Like Persona 4 Golden, this game has a cool split gameplay between fighting and daily school life however Persona 3 is the first entry to the series to focus on it.
Persona 3 FES is also home to one of the most amazing OSTs too. P3FES has one of the stronger OSTs of the series.
