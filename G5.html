Nier Replicant

  • Price: $60

  • Rating: 8/10 IGN

  • Publisher: Square Enix

  • Release Date: 23rd of April, 2021

Why Nier Replicant is one of my favourite games:

Nier Replicant, the remake of the spinoff game from Drakengard that lead to the hit success Nier Automata, has one of the most amazing soundtracks out of almost all games I've played. Nier Replicant is has incredible cutscenes with a revised combat system which feels cleaner and more fun to play than the original Nier.
The only problem I have with this game is its dreaded side quests and painful gardening mechanics but Nier Replicant makes up for it with its incredible graphics, soundtrack, cutscenes and story.
