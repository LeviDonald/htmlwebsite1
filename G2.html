Skyrim

  • Price: $70

  • Rating: 9.5/10 IGN

  • Developer: Bethesda

  • Release Date: 11th of November, 2011

Why Skyrim is one of my favourite games:

Skyrim, the 5th major addition to the Elder Scrolls Series, unlike its predecessor (Oblivion) it has much more improved graphics. However in my opinion, I find the Skyrim has worse writing than Oblivion, it does have its good moments with cool animations and a lot more budget and the insane amount of quests that are never ending in Skyrim make up for it.
Skyrim also is home to DLCs such as 'Dragonborn' which brings nostalgic omments for players who have Morrowind.
